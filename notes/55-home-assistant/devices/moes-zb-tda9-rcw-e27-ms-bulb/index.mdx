## Installation

### Introduction

This smart light bulb is Zigbee based, meaning all communication goes over the Zigbee technology and
protocol. It needs to be [paired](../../pairing/zigbee) with a Zigbee coordinator.

### Configuration

This device is plug and play. However, I had to retry pairing it 3 times before it was successfully
listed as "Supported".
